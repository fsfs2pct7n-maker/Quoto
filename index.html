How Quoto works
The smarter way
to hire a pro.

Quoto eliminates the weeks-long back-and-forth of getting trade quotes. AI analyzes your job, contractors compete on price, and you book in minutes.

1
Snap a photo & describe the job
Take a photo right from your phone or upload one. Add a quick description. No technical knowledge needed.
2
AI builds your job card instantly
Our AI analyzes the photo and description to generate a detailed scope of work, exact tools and parts needed, estimated hours, and urgency level. Every contractor quotes from the same card — no surprises.
3
Verified contractors send quotes
Licensed, vetted pros in your area receive the job card and submit their price, availability, and ETA — without needing a site visit first.
4
Pick the best quote & book
Compare price, timing, and ratings side by side. Book in one tap. A deposit is held securely — you only pay when the job is done.
Why Quoto beats the rest
🧠
AI-powered job scoping
No other platform generates a detailed parts list, time estimate, and urgency rating before a contractor ever sees the job.
💸
Real competition, real savings
Multiple contractors bid for your job. The average homeowner saves 20–40% compared to calling a single contractor.
⚡
Hours, not weeks
The average wait to collect 3 quotes the old way is 2–3 weeks. On Quoto it's under 2 hours.
🔒
Secure escrow payments
Your deposit is held safely. Contractors only get paid after you confirm the job is complete to your satisfaction.
